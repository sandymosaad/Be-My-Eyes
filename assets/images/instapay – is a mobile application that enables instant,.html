What is InstaPay?

InstaPay is an app that allows direct access to all your bank accounts and transfer instantly using your mobile device 24/7.

Limits & Fees

Learn More
  • your transfers are free of any applied fees.
  • Limits are defined as the following:
  • EGP 70,000 per transaction.
  • EGP 120,000 daily debit per bank.
  • EGP 400,000 monthly debit per bank.

    • What Is IPA?

    Learn More
  • An Instant Payment Address "IPA" is a simplified address for your account (name@InstaPay).

  • You can use IPA to receive money instead of sharing your account number.

    • How to receive money on your mobile number?

    Learn More
  • Your mobile number is associated with the account you have added to InstaPay.

  • If you have more than one account, you need to select a default account from account management.

  • This way you will receive the funds when someone sends money to your mobile number.

    • What is the transaction status handling?

    Learn More
  • InstaPay provides you with the transaction status instantly, the status can be one of the following:
  • Successful Transaction: means that the transfer amount is credited to the beneficiary account.
    - Rejected Transaction: means that the transfer was rejected, and the transfer amount is reversed to the sender account.
    - Pending Transaction: which means that the transaction is sent to the beneficiary bank which will executed it shorty. The transaction status will be updated shortly with either being successful or rejected.

    Sending Money

    Transfer to any bank card

    Learn More

    You can transfer funds instantly to the member banks using any card number

    • Click on Send Money
    • Choose to Any card
    • Enter the card number, card holder name & amount
    • Click next
    • Confirm the transfer details
    • Enter the IPN PIN to confirm the transfer 

    Request Money

    From other Instapay users

    Learn More

    Collect request:

    You can send a collect request to any Instapay user by using their Mobile number or IPA.

    • Click on collect money
    • Select the account number to receive the transfer on.
    • Choose to enter mobile number or IPA
    • Enter the mobile number/IPA 
    • Enter the amount
    • Confirm.

    To accept a collect request

    From the notifications/ pending request

    • Select the collect request 
    • Review the collect request details
    • Click on confirm 
    • Enter the IPN PIN
